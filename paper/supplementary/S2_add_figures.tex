\subsubsection{Descriptive Statistics}

Summary counts for the analysis:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Total street segments: $\nSegments$
  \item Total survey records: $\nSurveyRecords$
  \item Filtered trips (non-home destinations, purposes 2--6): $\nFilteredTrips$
  \item Total zones in survey: $\nZonesSurvey$
  \item Zones with both origin \& destination trips: $\nZonesBothOriginDest$
  \item Final zones in analysis (overlapping with street network): $\nZonesFinal$
  \item Mean zone area: $\meanZoneArea$~km$^2$
\end{itemize}

\textit{Note:} Closeness values appear small because they invert cumulative distances in meters (e.g., $1/28{,}000 = 0.000036$ for a 500m catchment). This does not affect rank-based (Spearman) correlations.
